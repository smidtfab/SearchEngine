Angie Arsenault Did It!

October 30th, 2010 Wolfman No comments

October 29, 2010…Angie Arsenault completed her 2nd Fan Funded project on Sellaband.com in a very exciting and tense final day.  In her first Fan Funded project, Angie raised $50,000 US dollars to fund her debut album, “Once Upon a Dream“.

With this, her 2nd Fan Funded Project on Sellaband, Angie will be using the budget to promote her music, including the new EP due to release in November, and the “Once Upon a Dream” album.  Specifically, the new budget will be used to:

1) Produce a “made for Youtube” short film style music video, featuring new music.

2) Hire a radio and press promoter for 3 targeted territories.

3) Online advertising campaign via AdWords and Facebook Ads

4) Online blogger outreach campaign

Enjoy this congratulatory video!

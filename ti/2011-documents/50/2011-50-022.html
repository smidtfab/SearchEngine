WHAT IS MECHANICAL TURK?
For those who don't know, Mechanical Turk, or MTurk, is a 'crowdsource' program where you earn dollars by performing basic tasks (known as "Hits") for employers (known as "Requesters"). You can be paid either in the form of cash (which Amazon pays into your bank account, or by purchasing Amazon gift cards which you can spend at their wesite. In my experience, Mechanical Turk can be one of the fastest ways to make money online.

Typical tasks that you might find on Mturk are simple, but ones that a machine is unable to do, for instance: rewriting sentences, completing surveys, writing original articles, copying text from scans and photos, transcribing audio files.

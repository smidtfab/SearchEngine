Top Ten Open-Source Collaboration/Crowdsourcing Traps to Avoid - NUMBER FOUR


Top 10 Open-Source Collaboration/Crowdsourcing TRAPS to Avoid

4: The "MORE THE MERRIER" Trap

Opening up your collaboration/crowdsourcing project to lots and lots of people may feel like the right way to go...but remember quantity does NOT always mean quality!

So, if you decide to throw the doors wide open...make sure you have the resources and the patience to cull through lots of BAD ideas to get to the GREAT ones.

At my company, we realized very early on that most brands need quality thinking quickly. So we increased the probability of getting just that by hand-picking the members of the BigHeads Network to ensure they are creative, influential, inventive, enterprising, imaginative...and proven problem-solvers.

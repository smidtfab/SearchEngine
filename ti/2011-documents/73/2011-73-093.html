Avi Wilensky, Founder, promediacorp.com. Tech geek.
  • Describe your task in as easy as possible terms for workers to understand the HIT instructions.
  • Be specific, and clearly express what is acceptable work and what is not.
  • Create a worker account and look for examples of HITs posted by top requesters such as Dolores Labs.
  • Inform workers how long it will take to get paid.

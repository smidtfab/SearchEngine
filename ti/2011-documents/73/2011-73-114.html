CrowdForce

CrowdForce, provides the tools and infrastructure that are needed to build, deploy and control a completely crowdsourced application, starting from scratch. Derived from CrowdForce, CrowdEngineering's products and services are a set of vertical implementations that address specific business applications. In particular CrowdForce lets customers:

  • Deploy and customize out-of-the-box, industry specific, applications such as Crowd4Care (crowdsourcing for customer care), Crowd4Sales (crowdsourcing for sales) or Crowd4Poll (crowdsourcing for surveys).
  • Create, deploy and customize, brand new business and consumer applications using workflow and application modeling tools, starting from scratch or from pre-configured workflow templates and services.
  • Integrate existing enterprise software applications built on top of industry's leadering technology suites (Oracle, Siebel, SAP, et al).
CrowdForce rapidly enables large and small enterprises to extend their outsourcing plans through harnessing the most skilled workforce, their Internet community. CrowdForce's processes and quality control provide a result comparable to internally managed processes, with a small percentage of the same effort

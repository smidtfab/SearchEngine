Chaordix Crowdsourcing Consultancy

With each client engagement, Chaordix provides a full range of consultancy and implemention services to ensure that your crowdsourcing program is designed to successfully achieve your business goals. Our SurePath™ implementation methodology guides a smooth process from beginning to results.

Whether you're seeking new product innovation, breakthrough problem solving or richer market insight, we make crowdsourcing simple, so you get the results you want. We guide the process, from planning to recruiting and moderating your crowd, to providing an optimized online experience for your participants, using methodologies that have been honed over time and experience.

Here's how we'll work with you:

SureStart™ – Crowdsourcing Planning for Success

Making crowdsourcing successful starts by setting clear goals and identifying the right approach. There are many ways of conducting crowdsourcing – no one model is right for all needs - so it's important to implement the model that will work best for you. Our SureStart planning process captures all salient information, ensures that all stakeholders up to speed on the opportunity, enables us to select the right model, and provides you with a clear written plan for the way forward.

The SureStart Process includes:

  • Stakeholder Crowdsourcing Orientation

    Crowdsourcing and open innovation can bring tremendous benefits, but also require a new way of looking at how your organization interacts with its employees and external crowds. To ensure all key stakeholders understand the full opportunity and the steps to get there, we'll conduct an online session to educate and answer questions to ensure everyone is on board.

  • Requirements and Input Gathering

    We'll quickly guide you through our requirements and input gathering process to capture information on your goals, target crowd, data needs, team and brand and technology requirements and roll them into our SureStart Plan.

  • Delivery of written SureStart™ Plan

    The SureStart Plan gives you a clear up –front view of how we’ll work with you for success. It includes such details as your target crowd including crowd profiles, incentives, participant roles and privileges, branding, schedules, needed outputs and awards of your calls to the crowd.

  • Best Practices Recommendations

    Great technology is only good if you have a robust crowd to use it and provide you with the insight and innovation you seek. We’ll explain to you our best practices on crowd promotions, recruitment & community management – how we will attract, engage and sustain crowd contribution.

Not even sure if crowdsourcing is right for you? The SureStart process is a great way to find out – we’ll deliver a readiness and opportunity assessment document that will help you determine for sure.

SureImpact™ Crowdsourcing Implementation

In the SureImpact phase, we’ll configure your customized online crowdsourcing site on the Chaordix Crowdsourcing Platform and work with you to launch your crowd! But that’s just the beginning: we’ll actively monitor your site and participation and schedule weekly or bi-weekly calls to advising you on any opportunities or concerns that we see. In short, we’ll steward your initiative to get you the results that you need – answers, intelligence and influence with your crowd.

Periodically, we provide a SureImpact Summary which includes:

  • Crowdsourcing results – outcomes, related comments, vote results
  • Actual activity vs. goals
  • Key learning – crowd behavior, effectiveness of incentives
  • New recommendations

SureModerate™ Recruitment & Crowd Management

Attracting an active crowd is the difference between successful and not so successful crowdsourcing. Even when starting with existing contacts, there are techniques that will best compel your crowd to get involved and stay involved. Our SureModerate™ services ensure your crowds are robust with:

  • Crowd Recruitment Strategy

    In the SureStart planning process, we identify who you want in your crowd. With this service we’ll plan a strategy for getting them involved. The written out put from this service will include where we’ll find your targeted crowd members, and the best ways to reach out to them.

  • Crowd Recruitment Outreach Services

    Chaordix Recruitment implements the recruitment strategy and involves live outreach to invite in crowd members in a variety of channels and methods, using online social networks and like-minded communities, relevant blogs and forums and online promotion and much more.

  • Crowd Moderation Services

    Crowd participation and the resulting output benefits greatly from live human moderation and persuasion to create the right conditions for people to contribute value, collaborate, and have a meaningful experience. Chaordix Crowd moderators have a unique skill set: part human catalyst, part referee, part cheerleader and part project manager. They’ll oversee all activity and help ensure your crowd is on track to deliver to your goals. Have internal resources you think have what it takes? We’ll train them how to do it.

Data Analysis and Reporting

Crowdsourcing provides a rich set of qualitative and quantitative data that can provide breakthrough insight into new business opportunities. At an agreed upon point in your program, we’ll provide a SureImpact Summary which includes:

  • Crowdsourcing results – outcomes, related comments, vote results
  • Actual activity vs. goals
  • Key learning – crowd behavior, effectiveness of incentives
  • New recommendations

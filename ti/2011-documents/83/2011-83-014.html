    Free concept for iPhone Weather App - Presentation Transcript

    1. Weather Pics A presentation of an iPhone weather application concept called Weather Pics (working title) By Roy Vink
    2. Weather Pics Why this app? Does the sun shine tomorrow? Is it really going to rain this afternoon? Everyday millions of people consult weather news and / or rain radars. This give us much information, but they are not visually appealing. This application makes the weather news visual! Presentation of an iPhone weather application concept called Weather Pics (working title) . B y Roy Vink. .August 16 th 2009. This presentation is for use of Meteoconsult only.
    3. Weather Pics How does the application work? The iPhone users do the work. Using the photo and gpsfunction of the iPhone, their weather photos will be placed as layer on the weather map and / or rain radar. Visuals of the local weather, shown in real-time! Presentation of an iPhone weather application concept called Weather Pics (working title) . B y Roy Vink. .August 16 th 2009. This presentation is for use of Meteoconsult only.
    4. iPhone users can make weather pics of clouds, snow, rain, sunsets etc. Location of picture known through iPhone gps function Weather Pics Function 1 The picture application Presentation of an iPhone weather application concept called Weather Pics (working title) . B y Roy Vink. .August 16 th 2009. This presentation is for use of Meteoconsult only.
    5. Photo taken by someone else, on other location around the same time. Search application based on date and time (see Starwalk app) Weather Pics Worldwide overview of weather photographs linked to the weather maps, radar or satellite images Function 2 Weather pics on radar Presentation of an iPhone weather application concept called Weather Pics (working title) . B y Roy Vink. .August 16 th 2009. This presentation is for use of Meteoconsult only. Photo will be automatically shown on weather maps. Also possibilities for 3D visualisation (using compassfunction)
    6. Function 2 Optional: Link with websites (or other iPhone apps) Presentation of an iPhone weather application concept called Weather Pics (working title) . B y Roy Vink. .August 16 th 2009. This presentation is for use of Meteoconsult only.
    7. All weather and cloud photos are automatically uploaded in a Flickr database Search option through existing functionality in Flickr Weather Pics Through Flickr possibility of election of the finest weather photo etc. Function 3 A picture search function Presentation of an iPhone weather application concept called Weather Pics (working title) .     8. Weather Pics End of presentation By Roy Vink

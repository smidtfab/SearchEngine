Project Launch: “Erster Weltkrieg in Alltagsdokumenten” – The First World War in everyday documents

24 March 2011, 3:10 pm

Press release by Europeana
Pictures, letters and memorabilia wanted
Berlin, 24 March 2011: “The First World War in everyday documents” is launched today with a call to the public in Germany to participate in building a digital European archive by contributing private memorabilia from the First World War.

Amazon Mechanical Turk is not a ptc site, but a task site. A place where you can earn from completing online jobs. Great for those who are looking for extra income.



How Does It Work?


First you will need to register at the site, if you have an account with Amazon.com, you can use the same sign-in for mturk.com. Later you will be prompted to register with Amazon Payments, which is Amazon's own online payment processor. You will use Amazon Payments to cashout your payment. Either to your bank or via Amazon Gift Card:


If a Requester approves a HIT that you've submitted, Amazon Mechanical Turk automatically transfers money from the Requester's Prepaid HIT balance to your Amazon Payments account. If you are a US based Worker, you can choose to disburse your earnings to your U.S. bank account or to an Amazon.com gift certificate balance.

Workers in India have the option of receiving bank checks denominated in Indian Rupees or disburse their earnings to an Amazon.com gift certificate. All other international Workers can only disburse to an Amazon.com gift certificate.


After you register you will be required to verify your email address and your phone. You can verify your phone via text message. 


After you have finished registering you can begin work. Click on the "Hits" tab and find a task that you feel you can complete or qualify for...





While you are learning how to use the site, start with the easy ones. For example "Find Product Information for Electronics (UPC)".


Basically you are searching the web for the UPC of a product and letting them know you found the exact product with the UPC listed. Be sure to check that the item is a exact match before submitting the UPC number. Some products will come in different models and sizes. See example of the task below:




Note that you will be in training mode when you start out, and they will give you a good idea of what to expect and what you did wrong if you completed a task incorrectly.



After you have completed your task and submitted it it will be submitted to the employer or what they call "Requester". If your work is approved then you should see your AmazonPayment account credited.



US members can cashout to their bank account if they have at least $10 in their AmazonPayment account. Or you can cashout by AmazonGift card program if you have at least $1 in your account.


India can cashout via check.


All others will have to cashout by Amazon Gift Card. Note that some countries will not be able to use Amazon for shopping so I have listed the countries that should be able to use this site and cashout via gift card.


Full Review


The site has been online for quite some time and is operated by a huge company, Amazon.com. A very popular online retail site. So it is not like they can't pay their bills...so obviously this is a Legit Site. Amazon ranks with Google, Microsoft and other big corporations out there.


We are adding Amazon Mechanical Turk (mturk.com) to our Legit List and possibly to our Elite List, after we receive our first payment.


Here are the pros and cons of mturk.com

 

PROS:

 


1. Paying - They are paying and payment proofs can be found on the web. Here are a few places where you can view these proofs:


http://amazing-life2009.blogspot.com/2009/04/amazon-mturk-mechanical-turk-payment.html

http://easyways-tomakemoney.blogspot.com/search/label/mturk

http://www.mylot.com/w/image/2381714.aspx



 

2. Instant Payments - For GiftCard it is instantly credited to your amazon account and you can redeem it easily online. Cashouts via bank or check (India) obviously will take longer.

 

3. Low Minimum Cashout - This site minimum cashout is currently at $1 for Amazon Gift Card. Which should be a easy target to reach and cashout with.

4. Established - It is Amazon, do I need to say more? A well established company.

5. Zero Investment or Upgrade - This is free to use and requires no investment. All you have to lose is your valuable time.

 

 

CONS:

 

1. No Referral System - Don't expect to earn by referring others. This site does not have such a program. 

 

2. No Forum - Their affiliate site has a forum, but AmazonTurk does not. Site does not require any investment, but it would be a nice addition if they did have a forum. Forums are a excellent tool for every site to have. Members can post proofs and have a open line of communication between staff and other members. This one does not have one.

 

3. Not Enough Work for Other Countries - I have read that US members seem to get more work than other countries. The same story I hear on many sites.  So I can't really say what other countries will see since I am in the USA.


4. Spam - Be careful with what tasks you accept and what info you give out. Some people have complained about getting spammed after completing a task from a employer. Which I wonder why they are using their main email address in the first place, and not a spam box. Never give out your main email to strangers. Rule number #1 for online safety.


5. A Virtual Sweat Shop? - Some have claimed that the earnings are too low and not worth spending your time on. In our view it is all about choice. You either choose to work for the rate given or you don't. I believe those who are use to using PTC sites, and are use to clicking for cents, will have no problem using this site. However those who are not use to such low earnings will not like the earnings and probably turn their nose up at it.


6. Complaints About Account Suspensions - Every site has complaints. This site is a huge site so there will be quite a few complaints. Some are from users who were suspended and said it was without just cause. You can find these complaints here:

Some Things You Should Know About This Site...

 

  • Taxes: "Amazon Mechanical Turk prompts you for tax information when you have reached a certain threshold in lifetime earnings. Amazon Mechanical Turk collects your tax information and provides it to Requesters in the event you reach the IRS tax reporting threshold with any Requester. They collect this information once, so you do not need to supply this information to Requesters. If you do not provide the required information, you will not be able to continue working on Mechanical Turk."

  • Bonus Payments:  "A bonus is an additional grant of money from a Requester. A Requester can choose to pay a bonus in addition to the stated reward for completing a HIT. Bonuses are granted at the Requester's discretion and are usually paid to Workers who do particularly good work"

  • Indian Rupees?: "If you are a Worker in India, you can elect to receive a check for your earnings in Indian Rupees. You will need to provide your birth date, passport number, Electoral Photo Identity Card (EPIC) number or Permanent Account Number (PAN). A scanned copy of your passport, EPIC or PAN card is also required. All the information you need to sign up to receive payments denominated in Rupees is provided on the Account Settings page.It takes 14 days to verify your card information once it is submitted. Once they have verified your information, you'll be able to withdraw your earnings in Indian Rupees. To request a withdrawal, go to the Account Settings page and follow the instructions. Your check is mailed within 6 weeks to the address they have on file."

Galaxy Morphological Types

Galaxies come in a myriad of shapes and sizes. In many (but perhaps not all) cases large galaxies are built through the slow build up of small galaxies into larger and larger systems. The way these galaxies combine leads to the standard family of morphologies: Elliptical, Spiral, Barred-Spiral, and Irregular.

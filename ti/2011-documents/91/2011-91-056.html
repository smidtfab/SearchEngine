Peekaboom

So I became very addicted to the ESP game after we learned about it in class..so I recently learned about new game created by the same people as the ESP game called Peekaboom. It is almost as addicting as the ESP game and a very entertaining.
Basically, there is two people (one "Peeker" and one "Boomer") and the Boomer gets an image with a word related to the image and the Peeker gets no image. When Boomer clicks part of the image, that part is now visible to Peeker. The Peeker has to keep guessing words related to the image by the portion they can see of the picture. For example, if the picture is of a fish and the Boomer reveals only the fin, and the Peeker starts to guess dolphin, ocean, shark, etc. until they get the word Fish. It is timed to see how many pictures you can get through.
There are other little catches to it like telling your partner if they are hot or cold, or giving hints through text, verbs, etc. if needed. Just like in the ESP game, partners can pass through hard images if needed. Its a fun game so I definitely recommend it if you are a fan of ESP game-its a little more tricky, but once you get the hang of it, there is not much of a problem.

Examples of barred (top) and unbarred (bottom) galaxies from Galaxy Zoo 2.
In doing this research Iâ€™ve learned that bars are really interesting features in disk galaxies. Unlike spiral arms, which are density waves (meaning stars pass in and out of them over the life of the galaxy), the matter in bars (stars and gas etc) actually rotates with the bar. This means that the bar breaks the symmetry of the disk of the galaxy and causes transfer of material both in an outwards along its length. What it boils down to is that bars should have a significant impact on the internal evolution of a galaxy.

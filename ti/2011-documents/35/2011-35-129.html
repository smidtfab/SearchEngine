Our client's most frequently asked crowdsource questions

    • How many logos will I get to pick from?You'll receive at least 40 unique logos to choose from. If you provide feedback or a larger prize, you'll receive even more.
    • How many designers will be working with me?Each contest receives dozens of unique designers from different backgrounds to provide you with a diverse portfolio of designs to choose from. To attract more designers, offer a higher prize & provide lots of feedback.
    • Can I ask for changes?Absolutely! We encourage it. Our professionals love the feedback. In fact, the more you provide, the more designers will get involved.
    • How much does it cost to run a logo design contest?You pick your price based on your budget! The more you offer our community, the more logos you'll get to choose from.
    • Will the logos be specific to my business?Yes. All logos added to your contest will be created specifically for you, according to the guidelines in your design brief.
    • What if I don't like any of my custom logos?If you're not completely satisfied, you can get a full refund within 60 days of your payment. You can also modify your design brief, extend your contest, or contact us for additional advice.
    • How long will it take?You pick the deadline! Your custom logos will all be entered before your deadline. You can get a logo in as quick as 1 week.
    • Can I show my entries to others, to ask for advice?Yes. We encourage it. A large reason our designs are so effective is they can be tested before you pick a winner. Ask your friends, co-workers, & especially your target audience to vote on which logo motivates them the most. Your contest already has the tools to gather this info.
    • Can I keep my contest hidden from the public?Yup! You can run a private contest that is only visible to our community & is hidden from search engines.

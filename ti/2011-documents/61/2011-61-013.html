So what is Article AutoTasker?

Article AutoTasker is a revolutionary new Windows® application which allows you to create and manage tasks (HITs) you submit to Amazon's Mechanical Turk online service (mturk) for the sole purpose of having articles written from scratch of having an existing article rewritten.

So what is Amazon® Mechanical Turk?

Amazon Mechanical Turk is a marketplace for work that requires human intelligence. The Mechanical Turk service gives people access to a diverse, on-demand, scalable workforce and gives workers a selection of thousands of tasks to complete whenever it's convenient.

Amazon Mechanical Turk is based on the idea that there are still many things that human beings can do much more effectively than computers, such as identifying objects in a photo or video, performing data de-duplication, transcribing audio recordings, or researching data details. Traditionally, tasks like this have been accomplished by hiring a large temporary workforce (which is time consuming, expensive, and difficult to scale) or have gone undone.

How does Article AutoTasker use Amazon® Mechanical Turk?

Article AutoTasker allows you to submit HITs to the Amazon® Mechanical Turk marketplace requesting new articles to be created based on specific keywords/topics or to have an existng article rewritten.

What is a HIT?

HIT = Human Intelligence Task. A HIT is a task which requires a human to answer. In this instance, we require a human (not a computer or spinner) to create a unquie new or rewritten readable article for us.

What are some of the features of Article AutoTasker?

Below are just some of the features which come standard with Article AutoTasker:

  • Multiple custom HIT type creation.
  • Two different types of HIT creation, New Article Creation and Article Rewrite.
  • Multiple custom request types for both new article and article rewrite HITs.
  • Single or mass import of HIT requests.
  • Automatic or Manual checking for article uniqueness using CopyScape.
  • Ability to Qualify workers by the number of HITs which have been accepted by other requesters.
  • Worker management - block/unblock workers, send messages to workers.
  • Plus lots more...

Will Crowdsourcing kill the news star?

Posted by gio (#1) 208 days ago (http://www.theaustralian.com.au)
Futurist Ross Dawson on The Australian about crowdsourcing's impact on newspapers as we know it today.

Mr Dawson predicted journalism would be "increasingly crowdsourced'' to "hoardes of amateurs overseen by professionals''.

Essentially journalism will be still there but roles of professionals and contributors will change in weight, timing and relevance of news will be considerably impacted by social networks and always connected device and all-day-nomadic "embedded" contributing journalists.

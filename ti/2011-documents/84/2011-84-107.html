Crowdsourcing Filmmaking: Follow Up On Iron Sky

Tweet


On 12.01.10, In News, Projects, Video & Rich Media, Video & Rich Media News, by Ricardo

We plan to follow up on the projects we talk about here on the blog, and today we come back with an ambitious crowdsourcing effort to get a fim done: Iron Sky, as it finally started shooting last week.

If you recall, they were looking to finance the movie through packages that anyone could buy and become a private investor (ranging from 1000€ to 5000€).

Recently, many updates were made available. One of them is quite another interesting approach: you can see the first 5 minutes of the film, and see how it was all made, and to do so you can choose to pay what you want, from 1€ to 100, “You decide the price” they say.

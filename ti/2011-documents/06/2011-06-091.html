Just Made Ten Cents on Mechanical Turk
I decided to check out MTurk, and just made my first ten cents.

Might be my last ten cents. What a bunch of lame attempts to get CPA offers completed (which I reported) and ridiculous requests:

- Write a 100 word unique article, using these keywords, about this topic, and I'll pay you two cents.

OK, maybe I'm being too harsh. My ten cents did take less than five seconds to make. Wasting an hour reviewing offers ... priceless.

Seriously, are people doing this stuff to make money?

Could be a great outsourcing tool, although I'm guessing many of the posters are as scammy as the requesters.

In an analogy to grid computing, HumanGrid has developed a "paid crowdsourcing" platform. Our goal is to effectively deploy human processing power as an on-demand resource for diverse kinds of business processes.

To achieve this, HumanGrid breaks down data-intensive projects into very small, self-descriptive tasks. These tasks are then offered to registered and authenticated Internet users (in essence freelancers) to be displayed within their Web browsers and solved.

Simple data-processing tasks are HumanGrid's initial focus. In the context of microcontracts, the freelancers see the instructions and the raw data that needs to be processed. They can then use the Web-based GUI to produce the desired results. After delivery and possible evaluation of the results, the freelancers are credited correspondingly.«Less

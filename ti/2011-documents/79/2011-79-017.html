About: CrowdFlower

An Entity of Type : organisation, from Named Graph : http://dbpedia.org, within Data Space : dbpedia.org

CrowdFlower was founded in 2007 by Lukas Biewald. to create tools to manage internet crowdsourcing. Crowdsourcing is ideal for large-scale, repetitive yet hard to automate tasks which require an always-on, scalable workforce to rapidly complete. To demonstrate the power of crowdsourcing, CrowdFlower created facestat, to show how a simple task can be quickly outsourced around the world

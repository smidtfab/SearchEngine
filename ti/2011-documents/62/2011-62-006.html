New Member: Marcin Wojnarski: TunedIT

Posted on March 23, 2011 by Brittany Lincoln

I would like to introduce Bart Blaszczyk of TunedIT to the Crowdsortium.

Our company TunedIT, was founded by Marcin Wojnarski, data mining scientist in 2009. TunedIT platform is the first online laboratory dedicated to intelligent algorithm development with the use of innovative crowdsourcing method – a new better way of outsourcing. TunedIT specializes in the fields of Data Mining, Machine Learning, Image and Video Recognition, Computational Intelligence and Statistical Modeling. TunedIT runs online data mining challenges that address selected real-world problems of data analysis. Competitions are open to the whole scientific community and attract hundreds of teams each time.

You can follow TunedIT at @TunedIT

Congratulations to our Graduating Students
MPA, MPP, MIAGE

Congratulations to graduating students in our Master of Public Administration (MPA), Master of Public Policy (MPP) and Master of International Affairs and Global Enterprise (MIAGE) programs.

Many of these students work in government and non-government agencies including: nonprofit, city, county and state governments, water and planning departments, public safety, federal government and more. Their dedication to enhancing their work through rigorous study and application will benefit the public by strengthening agencies that provide very important services.

We are very pleased to join these outstanding public service leaders in graduation ceremonies on May 7th.

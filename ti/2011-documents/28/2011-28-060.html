CloudCrowd Holdings, Inc.

SnapshotPeople

Company Overview

CloudCrowd Holdings, Inc. provides business process outsourcing services. It offers lead and database scrubbing services; processing scanned documents and archives; audio and video transcription services; content editing and summation services, such as rewrite of written information, review and grading of content, creation of document summaries and abstracts, and descriptions of products and services; and image evaluation services, including suitability review, trademark and copyright review, and pattern recognition. CloudCrowd Holdings, Inc. was founded in 2009 and is based in San Francisco, California.

Chaordix Crowdsourcing Software Platform

There is no "one size fits all" approach to crowdsourcing. Flexibility to apply different methods of crowdsourcing for different goals is essential to successful crowdsourcing.

The Chaordix Crowdsourcing Software Platform is an enterprise-class crowdsourcing engine on which we can quickly configure a powerful, customized crowdsourcing site that both meets your unique current needs, but has the flexibility to adapt to address crowdsourcing needs throughout your organization.

While many crowdsourcing sites may look alike, the real difference lies beneath the user interface. It's critical to understand how the technology guides an interactive process that encourages participation, provides moderation capabilities and ensures an unbiased outcome. Chaordix crowdsourcing software has evolved using crowd input from years of global crowdsourcing initiatives. Here are some of the top technical reasons that leading companies have chosen to work with Chaordix

Stimulated Idea Generation

We make it easy for your crowd participants to submit ideas and refine them so as to maximize the quality of the contribution by providing guided submission entry, the ability to attach files or images, and giving the idea owner the ability to modify their submission based on the crowd feedback and enhancements. We also enable three types of submissions – each designed to generate different business solution outcomes

  1. Brainstorm - freeform ideas. Great for: capturing new product & service ideas and enhancement and identifying new market opportunities, demand and trends.
  2. Solution Hunt - Challenge the crowd to solve more complex problems. Great for: R&D answers, crowd-created marketing creative, product design and engineering breakthroughs.
  3. Pick of the Best - Get the crowdâ€™s top selection from pre-determined options with insight as to why it is the best, and how to make it better. Great for: Market testing product design or marketing concepts and identifying best product, service or business ideas.

Contribution Evolution

Since most users in a community will not submit their own idea, but will comment and enhance ideas, encouraging commenting greatly increases the value of the crowd. Chaordix does this through a combination of incentives, an intuitive threaded comment display, and crowd management and communications capabilities designed to keep people engaged.

Unbiased Crowd Idea Selection

Having the crowd help prioritize or rank input is one of the biggest benefits of crowdsourcing. We enable participant voting and ranking of ideas through a choice of multiple voting models – thumbs up/down, arrows up/down, range, and single or multiple dimension scoring (i.e. ranking on multiple different custom dimensions for that topic). Voting can be weighted based on role and other factors to prevent gaming of the vote. Finally, we enable panel evaluation which allows experts or invited specialists to play a stronger role in final idea selection.

Engagement and Participation Incentives

Having a healthy crowd is one of the most important factors to great crowdsourcing output, so accordingly, the Chaordix Software Platform has many different capabilities to foster meaningful engagement.

  • Community tools allow individuals to create rich profiles and follow other users.
  • Customizable share & broadcast tools enable sharing, messaging and posting to blogs, twitter, facebook etc. Mechanisms to empower communication within the crowdsourcing community as well as externally to grow the crowd.
  • Motivation tools keeping the crowd motivated with incentives, awards and reputation management.
  • Dashboard and notifications keep participants in the know about new topics, hot ideas, new voting rounds, and make it easy to stay involved at their individual preference level
  • Self-moderation capabilities empower participants to help keep comments and ideas productive and ensure experience of trust and fairness.

Analytics & Answers

Access to reports and data give you the ability to glean the most insight from your crowd and maximize the return. We provide a set of standard reports that contain the most commonly requested, proven format output of submissions and comments, participant data and activity. We also provide customized reports and on-demand access to raw data in graphical and tabular form, for additional analysis or export to other analytical tools.

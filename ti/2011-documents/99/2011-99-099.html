Inspired by my crowdsourcing question, CNN anchor Ali Velshi talks to his viewers about their relationship

Stuck for a cool Sunday column idea yesterday, I came up with a quirky notion -- Why not crowdsource my Sunday column, asking the folks who follow me on Facebook and Twitter to answer a simple question:

What five things would you fix about TV right now?

One of those people, by happenstance, was CNN anchor Ali Velshi, who took time to write a passionate response he knew was too long to fit in my small Sunday piece.

So he told his viewers about it today, giving me a little credit for inspiring his words. I'm planning to post a lot more responses after the Sunday column runs, but consider Velshi's brief confessional a preview taste of what's coming.

And feel free to join the conversation yourself in the comments section below, if you're so inclined.

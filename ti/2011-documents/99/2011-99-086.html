Crowdsourcing for Innovation, Market Research and Brand Loyalty

Want faster innovation, more market insight or a stronger brand? Ask your crowds.

Crowdsourcing is powering better innovation, deeper insight and competitive advantage for organizations of all types. At Chaordix, we make it easy to get the full benefits of crowdsourcing by providing everything needed for success: we develop your crowdsourcing plan, recruit your crowd, moderate your process for best outcome, and provide best-in-class crowdsourcing technology that fosters collaborative innovation.

We work with companies around the world to develop crowdsourcing communities for co-creation, market prediction, testing and solving. Crowds are invited to submit, discuss, vote on, and often refine and rank contributions via the web, to efficiently identify the insight and answers that will help strengthen an organization.

Starting from your employee or partner contacts, existing online community or customer or subscriber database, you have the beginning of a crowd that can we can harness for collective wisdom. How can we help you?

CloudCrowd On the Move

My new favorite money-making site CloudCrowd.com announced that they have more paying jobs than their biggest competitor, MTurk.

Mark Chatow, the VP of Marketing and Business Development, made the announce in a press release in January.

Alex Edelstein, the CEO of CloudCrowd, said “we’re thrilled to have reached such a key milestone as quickly as we have.”

This is good news for all those those who want to get in on the bottom of a growing company.

Check them out at www.CloudCrowd.com

Our Client Promise

Our priorities are our clients. As a boutique software outsourcing house, we put our clients before everything else and we'd like to learn, understand and work closely on your projects. We bring the most value by meeting with you face-to-face either at on-site or at our offices. Our pre-sale consultants will work with you until we close a work contract for fully free-of-charge. If we cannot close on a work contract after initial consultations or your priorities change, we'll save your info in our files for future reference and/or same/other projects. Client relation and integrity is very important to us and you should check what our current clients are saying.

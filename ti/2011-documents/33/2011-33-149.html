Logolance to Launch Premium Freelance Logo Design Community

Chicago Startup Now Accepting Free Pre-Registration for Skilled Freelance Logo Designers

CHICAGO, IL--(Marketwire - December 20, 2010) - Today Logolance.com announced plans to launch a website dedicated to delivering premium freelance logo design to the world's top companies. Through Logolance, logo buyers will have access to work with a group of highly qualified designers on a single project, as designers collaborate and compete to create a successful logo design. The website will only host logo design projects over $2,000, and feature numerous rating and quality mechanisms that will reward highly skilled designers and eliminate those that don't produce excellent work.

Logolance will combine traditional studio logo design with the best aspects of crowdsourcing. Crowdsourcing is a new web phenomenon that taps the power of a collaborative community to solve a problem. In this model, both designers and buyers will work as a team to produce ideas, feedback, and market research in a way that was never before possible. Buyers will be asked to lend their opinion to other projects in exchange for free market research on their own projects. Likewise, designers will be asked to provide advice to their peers, and will be rewarded for producing quality design work regardless of whether their specific design is chosen as the final logo design.

"Our primary goal is to develop a website that quality designers love to use," said Michael Siegler, Founder of Logolance. "Our site will only host high-value logo design projects that can generate real sustainable income for designers all over the world. We want the best designers to call Logolance home, so we'll reward the best and let the rest work elsewhere."

Logolance is currently accepting pre-registration for designers that are interested in joining the community at http://www.logolance.com. Logolance will be a free service for designers. Early applicants can submit the URL of a webpage that demonstrates their work product for a chance to have some of their work featured on Logolance.com.

About Logolance.com
Logolance.com is the first dedicated premium freelance logo design website, connecting highly skilled freelance logo designers with the world's top companies. The website provides a platform for project management, collaboration, and market research combining traditional studio logo design with the best aspects of crowdsourcing. Through Logolance, buyers can work with a large group of designers on a single project, as designers collaborate to create a successful logo design.

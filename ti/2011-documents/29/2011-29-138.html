    Feb 11, 2010 7:25 PM

    www.crowdsourcingprofits.com Hello, this is Otis Collier, your Personal Branding Coach and I would like to take a few moments to tell you about a new web trend called Crowd Sourcing. This phenomenon is said to become one of the top ten marketing trends in 2010. Everything from political canvassing, software development, journalism, to environmental activism will be capitalizing on the growth of this model through the use of digital social media strategies. So what exactly is crowd sourcing? Well lets start with the word sourcing first. If you look up this word you will find that it has close ties with supply chain and recruiting. Sourcing in simple terms is the act of finding the right resources for the job that needs to be performed. In the supply chain world it could be parts or services needed to perform a task. In recruiting it is finding people who have the skills to perform a particular job. Crowd sourcing was first coined in June of 2006 in the pages of Wired magazine by writer Jeff Howe and editor Mark Robinson. It is nothing more than the compounding of the word crowd and sourcing. It is taking the tasks that are normally performed by employees and outsourcing them to a group of people. These people normally belong to a special interest group that work in partnership with each other around a common passion. The work is completed through the collaborative efforts of the group. http

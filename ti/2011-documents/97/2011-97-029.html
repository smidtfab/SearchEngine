Session Description

You read the crowdsourcing stories and have this nagging feeling that you're missing out.  But what about the whispers you hear "so and so's crowdsourcing campaign didn't work".   Reality is that there are secrets to doing crowdsourcing right.   Companies that know them reap huge benefits, companies that don't waste their time, money and risk their reputation.  This session will unveil those secrets and tell you how you can benefit.

Professional Biography

Randy Corke works with companies across the world in assessing their readiness for open innovation and advising them on the best ways forward.  Randy is a serial entrepreneur and a  member of the founding management teams of multiple successful companies, including several in the social media and mobile markets.

Amonng others, Randy was VP of Marketing for SoundBite Communications (IPO 2007) a pioneer in interactive voice messaging, and for Quack.com(acquired by America Online in 2000), which launched the world’s first voice portal. Corke holds a BA from Middlebury College, a MBA from Cornell University and has been a featured speaker at numerous industry conferences

How To Make Money Online Easily with Turk

By Russell Brooks

If you’ve got a sense of humor and don’t mind working for peanuts just to pass the time, you might want to try Amazon Mechanical Turk or simply Amazon Mturk. Mturk has been around since 2005 and though many negative reviews have come up against it from jobseekers balking at the ridiculous fees – pennies to a dollar a day - from job recruiters, some people have experienced decent pay just by working on multiple jobs. In fact, one has attested that she was able to earn something from $80-$100 after one week of making the effort to stick around Mturk during her spare time. While doing jobs, she was able to busy herself puttering around the house and running errands as well. For these reasons alone, Mturk could be just the ideal vehicle for you especially while America is still recovering from a recession.  

While it is interesting and fun, the operative word you should keep in mind is “easy” all the way. Amazon Mturk, in general, provides jobs you don’t have to sweat buckets over and presents a decent way of earning some money during you free time.

Still interested? Read on about how you can get on the Mturk express:

  1. Go to Amazon MTurk.Com and sign up to begin accepting jobs.
  2. Begin to browse the job placements from the HIT (Human Intelligence Tasks) pool, which were placed by Requesters a.k.a. employers.
  3. Click on the tasks you want to do while keeping in mind how much the Requesters are paying for the tasks you have chosen to take on. Some may be totally ridiculous pay-wise so just move on if you feel that it’s adding insult to injury. Just be reasonably selective.
  4. Don’t be surprised if you are asked to take a short qualification test prior to accepting a task. This is so the Requester can see that you are up to the task at hand. Be patient and wait for your so-called application to be approved.
  5. After a few days of taking jobs, you will basically have a feel of how the system works and which Requester you are most comfortable with. You are now past testing the waters. This is only the beginning. If Requesters can see that you are doing a good job, they will continue to get you and be willing to pay higher fees.
  6. That’s how this works.

Ideal HITs on Mturk for jobseekers apparently are for those with transcribing, typing, and writing skills. Transcribers get paid $0.75 for 5 minutes of audio. If you can transcribe and type 80-100 words per minute you get paid more. One of the best Requesters on the site is said to be “Casting Spells.” For writers with a good grasp of American English and grammar, Requesters pay from $1.00-$5.00 for a 200-500 word/article composition while those who are given the job of doing re-writes, the pay scale range from $0.04 - $0.06 per sentence re-write.

You can also answer and complete surveys that pay $2.00 apiece.

Some of the more mind-boggling jobs Requesters ask people to do for pennies is choosing a name for a baby, checking out a link that leads to a website, figuring out if two images are alike, reporting pornographic sites, and picking out an image for book.

Mundane and sometimes seriously creative tasks that eventually add up to the stash you have in your bank account.

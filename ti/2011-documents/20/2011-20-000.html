The Idea

A automated system that checks to see if someone is in your parking spot in the morning before you leave for work. ( THIS could be a small GPS device that is in the center of your parking spot or some sort of radio device that )
If there is a car showing up - The front desk of your office is alerted and security is dispatched. Also handy if any suspicious cars show up in your work parking lot after hours or maybe a home driveway.

You could be alerted via txt msg to cell phone.

Note: This type of software already is around with webcam home security systems.

I thought of this idea when I was...

I thought of this idea when I was looking at satellite images on-line.

( AT FIRST this was a satellite image idea but after lots of help from the crowd it works better as small gps units or some sort of radio transmitter, maybe even motion sensor cameras like current home secuity systems that use the web to alert home users. )

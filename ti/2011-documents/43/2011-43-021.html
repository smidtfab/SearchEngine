Announcement: Mechanical Turk releases new Worker management tools for Requesters
Posted By: Andrea@AWS
Created in: Forum: Amazon Mechanical Turk (Beta)
Status: Expired
Posted on: Oct 4, 2010 10:42 AM
Amazon Mechanical Turk has released a new set of features designed to help Requesters get better accuracy by providing tools to manage Workers.

Our goal is to make it easier for you to find Workers who are good at your HITs.

These new tools will:

1) Identify good workers by giving you data on whoâ€™s in your workforce and how they perform so you can easily.
2) Allow you to easily manage Workers and Qualification Types.
3) Save you time by providing information so you can prioritize your audit systems and send work to your best Workers.

The key new features being released today are:

1) Workers Statistics: Data on who is doing work for you, their performance on that work.
2) Qualification Types Management: Create new Qualification Types in the UI and assign them to Workers to create Qualified Worker groups.
3) Worker Management Tools: Bonus, Block and assign qualifications for a specific Worker.

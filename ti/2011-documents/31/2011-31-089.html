How To Crowdsource Creative Advertising

Innovation, Brand Marketing, Planning, Targeting
Ad Campaigns, Brand Identity, Crowdsourcing, User-generated Content
iMedia Connection
January 21, 2011

These days, you won't hear the word "gimmick" associated with crowdsourcing creative. Lots of brands do it. Some campaigns -- like Crash The Super Bowl -- are big and play to a national audience. Other campaigns are quite small and draw on an emerging group of creators, who range from passionate fans to freelance pros. Some vendors that specialize in crowdsourcing emphasize the ability to cultivate relationships with freelance professionals, while some brands use the technique as a wide-scale contest designed to attract a huge number of submissions. This dual approach has created some confusion between the idea of crowdsourcing and user-generated content (UCG).

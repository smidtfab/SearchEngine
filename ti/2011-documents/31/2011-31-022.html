Earn Money Doing Tasks With Amazon Mechanical Turk

Written by Sujan Mukherjee

Topics: Freelancing, Get Paid To Sites

Amazon Mechanical Turk is an Amazon.com company.A very reputed and popular marketplace of HITs or Human Intelligent Tasks that you work on to earn money.These can be of many different types from signing up with a newsletter to viewing ads on a requester’s website.

For Requesters

Requesters are the ones who place their works in the MTurk marketplace to be completed by the workers.It’s very good place to outsource your tasks.As a requester you are always on the safe side because you’ll have to pay only when you’re satisfied with the work.MTurk has a mechanism in place that make it easy to find the workers with higher accuracy rate.Accuracy rate is the percentage which tells you how successfully the worker completes the task.You can set certain qualification for the workers to do your tasks,and ensure you get your jobs done by the best of them.

For Workers

Thousands of people are the member of MTurk.There are certain positives and negatives of this great marketplace.

Negatives:-

Qualification

Most of the jobs in this marketplace does not require any kind of qualification.Anyone with some basic computer and internet knowledge can do them.But,the most lucrative ones in the marketplace are for those with some qualification.

Is it worth joining?

No doubt MTurk is very popular service among the requesters or employers,but,what’s in it for the workers.

Low pay rate

Most of the tasks are of very low pay rate.Some of them pay as low as $0.01.At best you can earn $10-$15 an hour.That’s just not good enough.

Requesters are the dictators

The whole system is very much in favor of the requesters.They can reject any work without showing any reason whatsoever.

No support for the workers

If there is any dispute between the worker and the requester,MTurk is not going to help you out.There is no guarantee that you’ll be paid.Your payment is solely depending on the goodwill of the requesters.If they do not accept your work they may not pay you.And you can’t do much about that,other than sending a polite request for reviewing their decision.

Payment Problem

Only the Indian and the US residents have the opportunity to get cash for their work.Other international members can only redeem their earnings for Amazon Gift Certificates.For Indians to get paid by checks in Indian Rupees,needs to verify their Identity by sending a scanned copy of your EPIC(Voter ID Card),PAN or Passport.This process takes bit of time.

Positives:-

They are backed by a company that is respected world wide.

You can easily find hundreds of work available.If you’re a skilled worker then,you can make good money with them.

Even if the pay rate is low,you get paid to do silly jobs like giving your opinion about a website,viewing ads and more.

In Conclusion

The idea of earning $5-6 per hour doesn’t interest me at all.And also the thing that me,as a worker does not have any guarantee of getting paid even after completing the tasks,never encourages me to work with them.There is no need to accept these things as  professional hazards.You can certainly find some better places to work.All you need is skills that can make you a very sort after freelancer.

I’ve shown you both the positive and negative aspects of MTurk.It’s up to you now,to join them or not.Before taking any final decision about them you may try them.Just see whether you can earn money with them or not.

Best of luck!!!

CROWDSOURCING FILM FUNDING

Crowdsourcing is becoming a popular form of media funding for recording artists, and now it is crossing over to the film world. The Biracy project is one platform that allows the audience to sponsor a film production, vote on key decisions, and share in revenues from film profits and friend referrals.

In an age of increasing piracy, Dave Geertz’s Biracy model supposedly puts the control back in the hands of the artist, although depending on how many crowd funded investors the film receives there may be many many cooks in the kitchen.

At the end of the day this is a unique trend in media financing and a model that could potentially be used to finance all kinds of art projects.

Limiting crowdsourcing to movies would kind of defeat the purpose of “outside the box” thinking.”

Turker Qualification Management

ScalableWorkforce.com is your control center for building and maintaining a trusted and qualified outsourced workforce – from turker qualification management to batch notification to incentivizing workers. By analyzing past and present job performance, you can make intelligent worker qualification decisions to get the right jobs into the hands of the right workers at the right time while validating the ongoing performance of each Mechanical Turk worker.

Managing a Qualified Workforce

HIT Batch Management

With Scalable Workforce, you can manage multi-HIT batches across a variety of job types such as data reconciliation, image verification, content rewriting, and data collection. Scalable Workforce offers consolidated views of HIT spend and job performance from a single application.

Mechanical Turk Job Posting and A/B Testing

The integration of Scalable Workforce and Mechanical Turk ensures your batches are seamlessly posted, tracked, and measured.  Scalable Workforce’s A/B platform enables users to submit multiple variations of batch titles, descriptions, and HIT instructions and smart serves the information to compute a statistically significant “suggested” job posting optimized to generate more worker interest, clearer job instructions, and better work production.  You can post a variety of job types such as data reconciliation, forum moderation, data collection, and web content development.

Tools to Manage Your Workforce

Email is the communication vehicle of choice for requesters and turkers. Scalable Workforce includes tools and templates to help build, manage, and track communications with individual workers as well as groups of workers.

Turker Quality Scoring

All Mechanical Turk workers are not created equal. Intuitive quality scoring features ensure your resources are spent on your most qualified workers.

Qualified Turkers

With Scalable Workforce, sharing qualified workers from one requester to another is a smooth process. Your internal staff may issue private worker qualifications or they may create a universal qualification accessible to your team of requesters.  Turker qualification management ensures that workers always receive the right jobs and never fall through the cracks.

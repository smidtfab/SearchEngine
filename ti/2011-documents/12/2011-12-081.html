    The Library of Congress - A Crowdsourcing Success Story

    "We invited your tags and comments and you responded. Wow, did you respond! Thank You.

    We placed only one tag ("Library of Congress") and two machine tags on each photo when we loaded them. Any other tags you see were added by the community; we are generally not controlling the content of Flickr tags, notes and comments. The identifying information is also appreciated--many of our old photos came to us with very little description." (library_of_congress)

    About 80,000 tags were added to the photos by 3,000 different people. Too bad the same thing can't be done on YouTube.

    The top 3 taggers are:
    1. Marshall Astor - 5,605 tags (Flickr profile)
    2. Emily Barney - 4,853 tags (Flickr profile)
    3. Harry Angstrom - 4,378 tags (Flickr profile)

    For more stats, go to Tracx » The Library of Congress › Flickr

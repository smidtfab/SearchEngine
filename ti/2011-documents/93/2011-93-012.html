Crowdbands, a crowd sourcing platform for bands

by Bálint Deáki on January 18th, 2011

Have you ever thought that you have an awesome idea for a favourite band of yours? I guess you did not really have a lot of options to share with them and even if you could on a band blog, they probably did not care about your opinion. With Crowdbands that could change.

Crowbands is a unique crowd sourcing startup for bands that allows music fans to have a role in creating their favourite band’s new album. For example fans can decide a new album’s title, which songs should the band put on it or help them make career decions and so on.

Fans can sign up for Crowdbands for $25 per year and take a part in decisionmaking processes. Basically the fans will have the role of an record exec while the Crowsbands site itself is the label.

The user can interact with their favourite band’s, help them building their careers and they will recieve the band’s album with exclusives like private performances and priority ticketing opportunities. For a real music fan this definately sounds good for $25/year.

Overall Crowdbands sounds like an awesome startup but in the reality, there aren’t many bands that signed up for it. If they can improve it in the future, Crowdbands could be a very popular service for hardcore music fans.

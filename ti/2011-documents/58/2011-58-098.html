DOD Runs Red Balloon Test Saturday:

Bob (K8GNZ) on December 4, 2009
View comments about this article!

The U. S. Defense Department is conducting a Communications/Internet exercise Saturday (12-5-09) that may be of interest to many hams. They will be tethering 10 big red balloons at undisclosed locations in the US.

The first individual to use all the communication avenues available to get the correct coordinates of the ten balloons will win the $40,000 being provided by the government.

We hams may be in a good position to "cash" in on this test prize with our repeaters, APRS, Echolink, Internet, etc. I've created a website with links to the government's announcement and details about this exercise at http://www.k8gnz.com/balloons and it gives some details about what I'm doing and some suggestions about what you can do.

This may be a good chance to really demonstrate what hams can do for public service if we can pull this off.

Better Apps With Crowdsourcing & The Cloud

A Joint Webinar with uTest and Skytap

Download slides for this webinar at SlideShare.net/uTest.

How Crowdsourcing and Cloud-Based Technologies Can Help You Launch Higher Quality Applications, Get To Market Faster, and Control Costs

We’re all trying to do more with less. For C-level execs and their tech teams, this means dealing with flat or shrinking budgets, while still trying to get new versions to market quickly, and maintaining app quality.

Now you can use online communities and cloud-based infrastructure to get the job done while reducing capital and operational expenses. Watch this webinar to learn:

  • How crowdsourcing and cloud computing can be used together to improve the application delivery process
  • Examples of how leading companies are using crowdsourcing and cloud-computing to achieve their goals
  • Best practices for using these solutions to launch higher quality apps and control costs

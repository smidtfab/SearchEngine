CrowdFlower was founded in 2007 by Lukas Biewald[1] to create tools to manage internet crowdsourcing. Crowdsourcing is ideal for large-scale, repetitive yet hard to automate tasks which require an always-on, scalable workforce to rapidly complete.

To demonstrate the power of crowdsourcing, CrowdFlower created facestat[2], to show how a simple task can be quickly outsourced around the world

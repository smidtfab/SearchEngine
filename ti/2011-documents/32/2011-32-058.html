Crowdsourcing Overview

Our Crowdsourcing services allow clients to get multiple versions of the copy they need in order to evaluate the perfect style, tone and overall feel of their messages.  It is a unique approach that helps to pin-point the perfect copywriter for each client project.  In most cases, the number of revisions needed drops significantly when clients use our crowdsourcing copywriting service.

What is Crowdsourcing?

Crowdsourcing is a way to utilize the ECOPYWRITERS network to produce multiple variations of an idea, slogan, campaign, product name or piece of content.  In other words, it helps you identify the perfect copywriter for your project.

How does Crowdsourcing Work?

You provide us with what you would like done and we can take care of the rest.  Our crowdsourcing technology platform allows us to easily access our network of copywriters and locate the perfect individuals to work on your project.

What kinds of projects should use Crowdsourcing?

  • Website Content
  • Product Naming
  • Slogans
  • Advertising Campaigns
  • Tag Lines
  • Radio & Television Commercials
  • Landing Pages
  • Splash Pages
  • Sales Letters
  • Product Descriptions

How Much Does it Cost?

Crowdsourcing services are usually included in the price of our copywriting services.  However, they can be ordered separately if need be.  The best way to find out more about our crowdsourcing capabilities is to contact an account executive who can assist you with pricing and receiving a quote.

Do you have examples of Crowdsourcing?

If you are looking for examples of crowdsourcing then please visit our Portfolio section of our site.  There you will find numerous samples of not only crowdsourcing but also examples of our other types of services and work.

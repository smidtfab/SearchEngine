Crowdsourcing: How to solve business problems with the power of the mass wisdom

without comments

The modern technology for communicating and aggregating what people know or think has become a powerful tool for business. Now organisations can break out of restrictions of sourcing ideas and solutions just within its full-time employed staff and appeal to the the worldwide community of experts.

For success of an outsourcing project the process should cover the three essential elements:
- Carefully selected participants to ensure that only qualified experts contribute.
- Diversity of members to include specialists from various geographical, vertical and functional backgrounds.
- Aggregation and quality control.

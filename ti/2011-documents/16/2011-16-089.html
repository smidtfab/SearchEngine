EventCamp 2011 Conference Crowdsourcing for Sessions Ends 1/21!

by Michael M. McCurry, CMP

As you may know EventCamp attendees have been reviewing and voting, for the past three weeks, on the education session proposals that were submitted for the 2011 EventCamp National Conference.  The proposal descriptions are listed at an official EventCamp website powered by CrowdCampaign,  Voting will officially end this Friday (January 21) at 5pm PST.

So, if you have not yet reviewed ECNC’s session proposals, please visit the website this week and cast your vote(s).

Thanks in advance for your participation!

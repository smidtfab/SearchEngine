Description

Welcome to the Galaxy Zoo app, where you can help astronomers explore the Universe.

The Galaxy Zoo files contain almost a quarter of a million galaxies which have been imaged with a camera on the robotic Sloan Digital Sky Survey telescope. To understand how these galaxies formed, we need your help to classify them according to their shapes - a task at which your brain is better than even the fastest computer. This app will download galaxy images and ask you questions about them, and then send the results back to our astronomers for analysis.

For more information, and a tutorial, see www.galaxyzoo.org.

Because of unusual technical limitations of the UK's Vodafone carrier system, this application may have problems on that network.

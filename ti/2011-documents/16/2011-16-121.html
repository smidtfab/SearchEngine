Companies beta > CrowdFlower

CrowdFlower provides Labor-on-Demand with always-on, scalable workforce. CrowdFlower’s solution allows businesses to instantly access thousands of workers and perform content moderation, business listing verification and sentiment analysis on large data sets. Customers include Forbes, Microsoft and Princeton University. CrowdFlower’s statistical quality control technology finally makes cloud labor a sustainable business reality through accuracy guarantees and efficient use of workers. This means results are high quality and arrive overnight. Try it for yourself at www.crowdflower.com

Specialties

crowdsourcing, sentiment analysis, business listing verification, content moderation, unstructured data, e-discovery, large data sets, translation

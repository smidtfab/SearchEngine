Who Should Attend?

Cloudforce 2 Singapore offers a valuable experience for:

  • CIOs and IT architects considering a cloud computing strategy and how to implement one
  • Sales and marketing leaders looking for best practices in lead and pipeline management
  • Customer service executives looking to modernize their call center applications and incorporate the social web into their multichannel service strategies
  • SME business owners looking to grow their businesses, and not their costs
  • Senior executives considering salesforce.com for the first time, or evaluating the return on investment from their salesforce.com investments
  • Project managers and business analysts working on CRM or custom application projects
  • Developers looking to build applications five times faster and at half the cost of traditional platforms while leveraging their existing skills
 

Why Should I Attend?

Cloudforce 2 Singapore is your opportunity to meet the salesforce.com executive team, to network with customers and partners, and to learn first-hand how businesses and executives across Asia are taking advantage of the cloud to deliver the business applications that they need in record time, with breakthrough results.

  • How is cloud computing changing the way people think about and pay for business applications?
  • What areas of your business can benefit from the cloud now?
  • How can I dramatically reduce the cost of innovation in my business?
  • How are others getting started, what issues are they facing, where are they finding success?
  • How can you get more value out of your salesforce.com relationship?
  • How do we shift the skillset and mindset in our business to move to the cloud?
     

These are some of the questions you may be asking as you look at your Customer Relationship Management (CRM) or cloud computing strategy. Cloudforce 2 Singapore is the place to answer these questions for yourself as you network with peers who have tackled these questions already and others who are wrestling with these questions right now.

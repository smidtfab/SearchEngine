The Multifaceted Advantages of Crowdsourcing for Companies, Organizations, Businessmen & Freelancers

Written on January 30, 2011 by Gman in Crowdsourcing Portals, Outsourcing Jobs, Social Media Marketing

Image Source

Crowdsourcing portals are smart marketplaces that can be very advantageous to both employers and employees (or workers, as they are called). It does not matter in which category you belong, if you know how to keep up a few standards, you will definitely find very helpful these online environments.

  • Smooth collaboration (between companies/organizations and workers) is the key phrase describing the benefits you can find on crowdsourcing portals
  • Profits increase for everybody – companies, individual webmasters, freelance workers

The main advantages crowdsourcing offers for businessmen, companies & organizations

Image Source

You visit these places as an employer, because you have several tasks to outsource. You’ll find here a marketplace with thousands of available workers, waiting to assist you with your projects. They complete tasks, and you pay only if you are satisfied with the result. Or workers bid with offers, and you have a free hand in choosing the one that you find most appealing.

Here are the main advantages for you:

  • Costs reduced with 50% or even more. Crowdsourcing portals are not renowned for offering high payments for the undertaken jobs. For a few cents of a dollar you can find writers, designers, freelance translators completing and delivering laborious tasks.
  • Reduced timeframe. If you need to proofread 10 pages by tomorrow, you will get it today. There are many freelance editors for instance who are seeking to make an extra income on these crowdsourcing portals. You just need to search in the right place, and let the right person come to you.
  • You ca find out what the mainstream wants. You have a few suggestions, but have no idea what logo to use for your company? Or which product has a greater selling potential?  Post surveys, pictures of your products, potential brand names or logos, and let people show you what they like, what they would buy.

Your main advantage as a worker on Crowdsourcing portals?

Image Source

Let’s not focus on the money here. Everybody knows already that you cannot get rich by categorizing products for one cent a piece on MTurk, or by completing surveys for 0.50 USD. Financial gain should not be your target when you join a crowdsourcing portal, but personal fulfillment should be.

If you are a talented translator, but you don’t have as many clients as you would want to, just start working on translation tasks. You can find translator requests on oDesk, Freelancer or Mechanical Turk. Don’t take into account the remuneration, just focus on delivering high quality work. Try one or two-times with the same employer, and if he likes your job, you might have got yourself a new client, outside the crowdsourcing portal. The same scenario is applicable if you are a designer, photographer, SEO expert, or programmer. Look at the whole venture just as if you would take part in an online internship program: minimum pay but offering maximum satisfaction to the employer in order to reach appreciation of your work.

Moreover, this is not a deal or a binding contract, so you can step in and out whenever you want to. Bored of working on the same repetitive multiple choice task? Switch, and look for something more interesting/ high-paying.

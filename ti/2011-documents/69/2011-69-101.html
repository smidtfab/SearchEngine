CrowdForge: Crowdsourcing Complex Work

+10-10
+ Bookmark Download Flag Email Add to favorites

Document summary:

In this research, the researchers presented a general purpose framework for accomplishing complex tasks using micro-task markets. Their approach is inspired by the MapReduce framework for distributed processing and provides a scaffolding for complex human computation tasks. They described
their general framework, a web-based prototype, and case studies
on article writing and decision making that demonstrate the
benefits of the approach.

Contributor comments:

The research makes three primary contributions:

1. Identifies the coordination requirements necessary to crowdsource complex tasks, and describe a framework to support a variety of task types.
2. Instantiats the framework in a prototype system for architecting the problem and managing the solution flow.
3. Demonstrates how the system can extend to more complex flows that include verification phases, and provide evidence that such phases can lead to better outcomes. It also describes how the system can be extended to support arbitrarily complex and recursive flows.

The Winner: Hugo Louroza

If we learned nothing else from the Gap logo debacle of 2010, it's that you can't touch a logo without first consulting the Crowd. After issuing a challenge to redesign our logo on 99designs.com, an online community of graphic designers, we received 627 entries.

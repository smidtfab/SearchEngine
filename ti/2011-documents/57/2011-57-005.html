Hello everyone,

Has anyone tried to use custom theming in reCaptcha Plugin (http://www.grails.org/ReCaptcha+Plugin)?
Does recaptcha:recaptcha tag supports custom_theme_widget attribute?

Will be very grateful for any simple example.

Thanks,
Taras
Taras Matyashovsky

Re: reCaptcha plugin and custom theming.

Reply | Threaded |  
Finally I get it work:
    <div id="recaptcha_widget" style="display:none">
           <div id="recaptcha_image" style="width: 300px; height: 57px;"></div>
           Enter the words above:
           <input id="recaptcha_response_field" name="recaptcha_response_field" type="text" autocomplete="off"/>
        </div>
       
        <recaptcha:ifEnabled>
          <recaptcha:recaptcha theme="custom" lang="en" tabindex="5" custom_theme_widget="recaptcha_widget"/>
        </recaptcha:ifEnabled>   

If someone has another example, please share.

Regards,
Taras

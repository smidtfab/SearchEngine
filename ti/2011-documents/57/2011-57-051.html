Blog entry:Austin Day 4: Learnin' somethin'

Apr 1, 2011


Annie Shreffler, World Compass

Annie Shreffler is a Boston, MA-based World Compass Staff Member and...

To give you an idea of the scope of SxSW Interactive and how much discussion took place about the impact of the internet on our daily lives, I should give you an idea of how many choices there were for attending sessions. Seven hotels across downtown Austin and two conference centers all hosted sessions between 9 and 5. They hosted talks by category: Social Broadcast, Future of Journalism, Meet Ups & Podcasting, Social Graph, Late Break Programming, Work & Happiness, Business & Education, Latin American, Health, and Robot Programming.

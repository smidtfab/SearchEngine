Custom-Made Logos VS Crowdsourcing – The changing trends!

Categories: freelance logo designers, logo design contests, logo design tips
Written By: Nora Reed
The subject of today’s discourse is a very controversial but critical topic in the contemporary logo design world. Yes folks, it’s the changing preference of logo design companies from custom-made logos to online logo design contests. Since its inception, online logo design contests have created a profound impact on the logo designing marketplace. I will be making an analysis on how the evolution of online logo design contests has changed the client preference of custom-made logo designs.

The best way do a comparative analysis is to chalk out the merits and demerits of the subject under discussion. Let us discuss the advantages and disadvantages of both custom-made logos and logo design contests.
 

Reasons of this Major Change in Logo Design Industry:

Below are few reasons which have made “Crowdsourcing” so lucrative and appealing.

  • It provides a premium opportunity for all designers to earn a living.
  • Graphic Designers can build their portfolio by participating in logo contests.
  • Crowdsourcing is economical and affordable for small business.
  • Through Crowdsourcing, logos are designed in a matter of hours.
  • Crowdsourcing provide designers with adequate exposure.
  • A client gets more than 50 logos to choose from.
  • Designers can participate in as many projects as they want.
 
The biggest evidence that bears testimony to the growing demand of logo design contests is the shift of logo design companies from custom-made logos to logo design contests. The most recent case in point is one of the prominent Logo Design Companies …Logo Design Guru. This proves the success and growing trend of crowdsourcing. So I would advice all aspiring and talented designers to participate and earn you worth on sites like Logo Design Guru.

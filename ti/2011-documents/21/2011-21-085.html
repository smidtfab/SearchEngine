Welcome

Welcome to Team DeciNena 2009

Update: We lost. Team MIT Media Lab's Pyramid Scheme/MLM tactic seems to have won.

We filed our entries, and had 9 of the 10 balloons right, but we knew our #8 entry was sketchy despite assurances from other teams we acquired it from.

We were still trying to trade for a legitimate #8 when we noticed the DARPA site no longer said "No Winner Yet" but we kept going.

We now have a List of all known team Post-Mortems up.

We are going to keep our team together and the site up in case we want to do any other challenges like this (or in case DARPA runs it again next year in some form). It was great fun and we learned some cool stuff.

At 5PM Mountain Time Sunday Dec 6th we held a Post-Mortem Public Chat. Members from a significant number of teams attended and asked and answered questions. A transcript is now available.

We are one of several teams who competed in the
DARPA Network Challenge on Dec 5th 2009.

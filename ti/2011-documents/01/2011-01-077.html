Can Companies Obtain Free Professional Services through Crowdsourcing?

+10-10
+ Bookmark Download Flag Email Add to favorites

Document summary:

An in-depth discussion on whether companies can truly benefit free professional services through crowdsourcing.

Contributor comments:

A for-profit company may not be able to harness the energy of worldwide crowds to obtain free services. However, companies whose users have that rare motivation may gain various benefits from crowdsourcing, such as speed and a more loyal fan base, even if the service is not actually free.

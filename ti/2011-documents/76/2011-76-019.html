Make Money Online at Amazon Mechanical Turk

Feb 18th, 2010 by Liana Thomas

Learn how to make money online using Amazon's Mechanical Turk.

The Amazon Mechanical Turk is a program, which allows users to create either a requester or a worker profile. The jobs on this website are called hits and they are how users will make money online. Hits are various jobs, which may include writing short articles, taking research surveys, signing up for a program, or any number of other simple tasks. The hit’s description will provide information, such as how much the worker will be paid for completing the hit, how much time they have to complete it once the hit is accepted, and what needs to be done in order for the hit to be approved, rules which the worker will need to follow precisely in order to earn money online with that hit. Some of these hits even offer bonuses, which allows the workers to make even more money for excellent work.

A requester is a person or a company that places the hits on Amazon Mechanical Turk and allows people to make money online through these hits. If they need a task to be completed, then they will post it in the hits section of the website and wait for people to accept and submit their work. If a hit is poorly completed, then the requester has the right to reject the submitted work and refuse to pay the worker. But, if the worker follows the instructions precisely and has obviously put effort into their work, then they will paid and the requester may even pay a bonus for a job well done. Also, if the requester is very impressed with the work, then the worker has a better opportunity to earn money online because the requestor may contact the worker directly and offer that person the opportunity to do more work for them.

The workers are the people who make money online through this website. As a worker, you can search for hits containing specific words or phrases of your choice and  you may even narrow the results down by choosing a price range. The results can be sorted by choices such as their creation date, reward amount, and several other options. If you don’t put enough effort into the work you do for the requestors, then you could get rejected for payment and if you break a major rule, such as plagiarism, then you will not only be rejected, but you may also be blocked by the requester and wont be allowed to do any more work for that person or company, which will significantly lower your opportunity to earn money online through this website.

The dashboard is an area where you can view how much money you have made, along with other details such as accepted hits, submitted hits, rejected hits, and several other details. Amazon Mechanical Turk is a great and fun way to make money online, but if you get rejected by a lot of requestors then your approval rate will go down and it will be harder and harder for you to earn money online through them. When you feel you have earned enough money that you want to take it out of your Amazon’s payments account, then you can either transfer is to your bank account or have it transferred into an Amazon Gift Card, which you will be able to use to shop online at Amazon.com.

Using Mechanical Turk to Jumpstart Your Startup (Lior Ben-Kereth, face.com)

Thursday, April 7, 2011

4:30 PM

Selected By: Micha Porat

The Junction

32 Schocken Street Tel Aviv

Selected By: Micha Porat
60 Entrepreneurs going · 35 Entrepreneurs waiting
Micha Porat (Co-Organizer) added this Meetup on Mar 27, 2011

Who's hosting? Micha Porat

While technology continues to improve, there are still many things that human beings can do much more effectively than computers, such as identifying objects in a photo, performing data de-duplication, transcribing audio recordings or performing research. The Mechanical Turk platform enables companies to programmatically access a diverse, on-demand workforce to accomplish these tasks (and more) quickly, accurately, and at a low cost.

We’ll talk about:

  • What is Mechanical Turk? What is it made of
  • 3 Ways to use it: SDK, UI, CLI tools
  • How face.com use it to find a needle in a haystack
  • What we learned: Optimization, tips, dos and don’ts
  • Examples

Welcome!

The Junction http://www.thejunction.co.il is an open house for entrepreneurs. Any active entrepreneur (regardless of his/her idea) is welcome to join, be a part of, and work at The Junction.

We believe that The Junction will be more than just a work space for entrepreneurs. It will be a community of creativity, productivity, and innovation. It’s a place where we know magic will be created. To help foster this innovation, we will be holding events at The Junction, for which this meetup group was created. We welcome all entrepreneurs to join us in these events.

The Junction is sponsored by Genesis Partners, an early stage venture capital firm as part of our collaboration with the entrepreneur community.

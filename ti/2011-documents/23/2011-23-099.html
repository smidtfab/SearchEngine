DARPA red balloon Network Challenge

December 15, 2009 - 3:14pm — 1450

Submitted by Carl Anderson

On December 5, the DARPA red balloon "Network Challenge" took place.  
Groups around the nation participated as social networking groups to find 10 tethered red weather balloons across the US.  I was monitoring a team sponsored by the Georgia Tech Research Institute from a cross country flight.  OK, I was not really looking for red balloons from the airplane, I was watching the website and watching http://twittermap.tv/ with the search word Balloon.  The twitter activity was interesting to watch with people in Europe and worldwide were participating in the chatter.

Congratulations to the MIT team for winning and DARPA for this challenge.

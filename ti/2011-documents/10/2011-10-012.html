Logogenic Reveals A New Logo Design Crowdsourcing Platform.

Logogenic provides a global crowdsourcing platform for small business looking for cost effective ways to get a logo designed. Clients can host a project on logogenic.com and receive 30-100 entries submitted by a talented community of logo designers
 

LG_PR_image

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

PRLog (Press Release) – Mar 08, 2011 – Logogenic.com is an affordable solution for small business to receive amazing graphic design services. Logogenic utilizes the power of the crowd. Rather than one or two designers working on a project like a traditional design firm, logogenic uses a large community of professional logo designers worldwide. You could have anywhere from 30-100 or more designers all working on your design project.  We make it easy to run a project on logogenic.com for both beginners and seasoned clients.


Gone are the days of contracting with one design company and waiting weeks or months to see a paltry handful of similar-looking professional logo designs. Logogenic is the smart, affordable way for small businesses and startups of all stripes to choose the perfect professional logo design from numerous options tailored to their unique specifications.

Logogenic’s cutting edge crowdsourcing platform allows your company to utilize the artistic talent of a virtually unlimited number of graphic designers around the world. Instead of being restricted to one design company’s visual ethos, Logogenic affords you the opportunity to choose from up to 100 or more singular logo design concepts at a fraction of the cost.

Logogenic understands that new businesses sometimes have tight budgets. The model we use to solicit logos is much more affordable than the conventional method of paying a set fee to one contracted design company up-front.

Forget about waiting prolonged amounts of time for designs on a business week schedule. With the Logogenic platform, as soon as you launch your project, designers will start working on and submitting potential logos. The durational drawbacks of a traditional 5-day work week are completely obliterated.  Your small business or startup will receive 30 to 100 proposals within a few days, a significantly shorter time span than traditional design firm models.   Once you’ve chosen a design, you receive full legal ownership.

Logogenic provides an affordable, simple way to tap into a global pool of graphic design talent. Visit logogenic to begin the easy, intuitive process of crowdsourcing a professional logo design that flawlessly represents your business.

What We Do

Crowdsourcing is the act of trusting a project to a large, disparate group of people (aka “the crowd”). Under the right conditions, the crowd will complete the work better than a specialist or an expert. Work that once took months can now be done in days — with the same level of quality.

CrowdFlower is the industry leader within a specific segment of crowdsourcing, often referred to as Labor-on-Demand. Labor-on-Demand is especially useful for large-scale tasks that computers have difficulty handling, but people do well.

Some ideal tasks include:

  • verifying information
  • categorizing images and text
  • assessing relevance
  • enhancing data records
  • and more that we're discovering every day.

We offer the accuracy of a trusted internal workforce that can scale up or down faster than a traditional outsourcer.

How We Do It

CrowdFlower takes large, data-heavy projects and breaks them into small tasks that we distribute to an on-demand workforce around the world. Our technology then aggregates the results and controls for quality.

Whether performing data analysis, tackling complex workflows, assessing worker competency, or even optimizing price structures for our clients, we take a scientific approach to everything we do.

Our technology platform encapsulates this approach. It has been refined by over 85 million judgements and is currently producing solutions for some of the world's largest companies.

Who We Are

Formerly known as Dolores Labs, CrowdFlower was founded by Lukas Biewald and Chris Van Pelt in 2007. CrowdFlower is headquartered in the heart of San Francisco's Mission District.

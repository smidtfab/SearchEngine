My Expert Tips On Earning More From Mturk As A Worker

Hi!

Some of the readers have emailed me enquiring how can they earn more from Mturk. Well, I thought, since I have already stopped working at Mturk from last few months due to reasons listed at:

http://www.pctipstricks.com/my-review-of-amazons-mturkcom-after-working-for-them-part-time-for-few-months-as-a-worker/

So, why not share the tips that will probably help people in making more money. I am sure that some of these tips will definitely help  you – specially new workers as well the experienced ones too. So, here are my ideas that may help you in earning more from Mturk (not sure if you can do it as the Mturk HITs have reached disgusting low levels – checked from Net):

- To get eligible for more HITS, clear the qualification tests (Check Qualifications tab by clicking on it).

- If you are new to Mturk, then start with the HITs that are easy and available most. The idea here is to raise your HIT approval rate (%).

Once you have high approval rate, you can go for slightly difficult ones and earn more. Always maintain a high approval rate as quality Requesters mostly keep HITS approval rate (%) high to only let the best workers work on their HITS.

- Make sure that “Show all details” is clicked when working on HITS (in all open HITs browser windows), this will aid you in seeing the vital requirements and qualifications for that HIT.

- Always use a multi-tab browser such as Firefox so that you can open the different types of Mturk HITS in various browser windows. Clue – one tab each for Reward Amount (most first), HIT Creation Date (newest first), HITs Available (most first), Expiration Date (soonest first/latest first and Time Allotted (most first/least first). These browser tabs for HITs are arranged as per my preferences, you can choose a different order of the browser tabs as per your preferences.

You should definitely keep on refreshing your browser windows in these tabs to keep a check on the HITS and then clicking at the fastest speed to get the interesting ones matching your choice. Oh! needless to say, you must first see if you can do the HIT and the first choice must be to get the HIT of highest reward.

- Writing and transcription HITS as observed by me used to pay more, so try doing these HITs. Or, check the HITS that pay more over a period of time. If you are not able to do these high paying frequent HITs because you don’t have the skills then get those skills. But make sure to analyze over a period of few months that these types of HITs frequently comes at Mturk as you might waste your money in acquiring these skills unnecessarily. I think that the chances that your skills will go waste are remote as you will definitely get work from some other sites also. Skills never go waste if you keep on looking for ways to utilize them somewhere.

- Use a good fast broadband connection so that you get the HIT when you click on it. If you are clicking on a HIT and getting a message that the HIT has been assigned to someone/Not available then you must check if you are clicking on the HIT quickly or should go for a faster broadband connection. 2 Mbps broadband connection should be Okay but if all workers have the same broadband speed connection and they all are clicking at a particular HIT at the same time, the one with the faster broadband speed and PC wins. So, decide on this point accordingly as per your experiences at Mturk.

- If a Requester rejects your HIT, contact him/her and ask the reason. I did get some of my HITS approved this way. Contacting Mturk is useless in such cases as they are only interested in their commissions from Requester and not in getting your money from Requester. They give a shit about Worker’s money (it hurts but it’s true).

Also, on the other hand, appreciate a Requester who approves all your HITs and pays quickly. Write and maintain a contact with these Good Requesters. The probability is there that the Requester may choose you and other similar quality workers exclusively for future similar HITS at the same rates. Also, they may give you the work directly without Mturk if they find that the work requires skilled workers who are already in touch with them through email. What is the point for a genuine Requester to get the HITS done through MTURK if the same workers are directly available to do the same work at the same rates minus the Requester commission paid to Mturk? Requester in such cases will thus save their commissions other wise given to Mturk for posting the same HITS there.

Also, sometimes, the instructions given by the Requester in the HIT are not 100% complete (despite their best attempts). When you contact such Requester after your HIT work is rejected and correspond with them, they take care to relax their approvals accordingly and also take care to include full details for the future similar HITs posted at Mturk.

- Join Mturk workers forum – http://turkers.proboards80.com and be active over there. It’s a nice forum where your peers, working for Mturk HITs, post their opinions, problems and solutions etc related to Mturk HITs. No body can give you the real, quality  money making Mturking tips close to turkers.proboards80.com – Mturk workers community!  The reason is the volume of knowledge that comes  through hundreds of the Mturkers sharing their experiences as a Mturk worker.

Another forum of Mturkers worth joining (some what at small scale presently as compared to http://turkers.proboards80.com) is http://www.mturkforum.com/.

The registration for both of these Mturk workers forums is free and you can definitely increase your earnings by being a committed member over there. Examples of what you can gain from these two forums – List of Requesters that don’t pay at all or do illogical rejections, Requesters that are open to feedback of Workers and pay honestly, Spammers, tips for getting HITs approved etc. I hope you got the clues for how being active at these two forums can increase your Mturk Worker earnings. I always write Worker earnings specifically as there are Requesters at Mturk who are also earning by not paying to the Workers despite their 100% correctly submitted work.

Hope that these tips sure-shot help you in earning more from Mturk.

I wish you all the best in pulling more money from Mturk!

Cheers!

rajeshmago May 28th, 2009 at 2:55 pm

@ Roshan: Good question. Mturk scripts are instructions installed in the Worker/Requester browser to aid them in working/posting and designing HITS respectively. Personally, I have never used scripts at Mturk till I last worked in November, 2008.

I researched and found that there are good and bad uses of scripts. Good, when you are using it for helping yourself in saving your time, for example – A Red indicator showing that you have an assigned HIT to be completed. Bad when you are using it to auto complete the HITS without checking the HITS at all. Example: A HIT that requires you to fill in three tags depending on the given content. The user using the relevant tags filling script doesn’t even read the paragraph and let the script fill the data automatically leading to irrelevant tags sometimes.

Mturk has banned some of the users using scripts or/and bots (instructions/programs used for completing a task by automating it to a large extent as possible) for programming the completion and submitting of the HITs.

More examples of scripts that could help Mturk Worker (I have only chosen only those that I think will not cheat the Mturk system but would save worker’s time) are:

- Turkopticon scripts let you see the review of the Requesters (if available from Workers) posting the HITs. This can definitely help you in working only for the Requesters who have good reviews. The review of the Requester is shown by a Button (only for the Requesters with some review data), which is filled with a color – the color depends on the type of Requester, example – Red for the Requester who doesn’t have good reviews etc.
- Displaying current earnings to your Worker account
- By Installing Accept/Skip script, the user can simply press A or S for Accepting or Skipping a HIT respectively.

And much more…

For more details, refer to the following URL’s:

http://turkers.proboards.com/index.cgi?board=kscripts&action=display&thread=4223

Alex Edelstein (born January 17, 1969) is a high-tech business executive and reality television participant.

Career

Alex Edelstein is the Chief Executive Officer of CloudCrowd a company in the crowdsourcing space that he co-founded with Jordan Ritter in 2009. He also heads EditZen and TranslationZen1 Before CloudCrowd, he was a real estate developer and the head of Gemstone Development, where he built the Manhattan residential condomium project 2 He previously held executive roles at Cloudmark and Inktomi. He product managed the early versions of Netscape Navigator.3 Additionally, he was on the design team for Microsoft Exchange and Microsoft Outlook.

Reality Television

On April 2, 2009, he was featured on the reality TV show The Millionaire Matchmaker. 4

Community

He provides television analysis on both real estate 5 and high-tech topics,6 and is on the Board of Directors of Kiva.org, a non-profit company that facilities micro-lending in developing countries.7

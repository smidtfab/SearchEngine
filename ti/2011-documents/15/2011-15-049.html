Virtual Workplaces

They connect online job seekers with providers. This is a good way to earn a large amount of money, but you need to work hard for better earnings.

I was very interested to these jobs past, but now with my lifestyle I do not have enough time to spend in these workplaces.

During my vacation period, I've earned over 550$ per month after a massive hard work. Don't be surprised, some people earn 800-1000$ or over per month!

All of following job registrations are free.


Amazon Mechanical Turk (www.mturk.com)

(1) This site belongs to giant online company Amazon.com.

(2) Every time there are over 80,000 HITs (Human Intelligence Tasks - simply Tasks) available.

(3) You can select various and a lot of HITs of your choosing according to your abilities and knowledge.

(4) Also there is a qualification system. You can apply various qualification tests, with better qualifications you will be able to aproach highly paid tasks.

(5) Always provide quality works to approval. If not your works will be rejected.

(6) Maintain higher approval rate. It helps to qualify additional highly paid tasks.

(7) Payments via Amazon. No payment threshold.

My rating: 100%
My recommendation: Good site



Short task (www.shorttask.com)

(1) It is a better alternative to mturk.com.

(2) Website environment is more than 80% similar to mturk.

(3) Every time there are over 20,000 tasks available.

(4) Payments via paypal. Payment threshold 10$.

My rating: 80%
My recommendation: It's free, try it!

How to make money with the Amazon Mechanical Turk

58
rate or flag this pageTweet this

By barryliam

Mechanical Turk?

The amazon mechanical turk is purely this " a crowdsourcing marketplace that enables computer programs to co-ordinate the use of human intelligence to perform tasks which computers are unable to do" and translated to english that means its a site you go on to do easy jobs like renaming videos. the amazon mechanical turk really is only that but its earning potential is vast. The site usually has around 50,000 jobs at a given time to be done which offers plenty to everyone thats interested with more added each day. The tasks are reasonably easy and only a few require research work to be done.

How Much?

Right so lets get down to the nitty gritty, i would say on average most people earn $0.15 a HIT (Human Intelligence Tasks) although some hits pay around $2 - $10. considering these hits only take around a minute or so to complete and earning $0.15 a hit you could make a nice monthly income, if you did around 200 a day you could earn over $400 dollars a month, it really is up to you how much you would like to earn. Personally I only do around 20 a day for an extra $60 a month which is fine for me. The only downside is that you may find yourself doing repetitive tasks, But you could think of this as a plus because the more you do the task the quicker you become resulting in more money.

Qualifications

To complete some HITS you must have a certain qualification but dont worry you can take a quick test which should be offered to you next to the HIT, the tests are reasonably easy and are only there to see if you are able to do things such as renaming a video or placing an amazon item in its proper category.Â

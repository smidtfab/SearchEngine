Veikkaus Oy- Finnish Lottery

Service: Activator
Veikkaus Oy- Finnish Lottery wanted to support it's strategy planning process by increasing knowledge and certainty on what interests young adults in lottery games, sport games, instant games and other draw games and entertains them.
In a few weeks we got over 800 participants and we activated the crowds by movieticket draws. The results reaffirmed our views and encouraged us further define our processes in accordance to the wishes of the participants. We will further go through all the comments and use them in both our strategic and operative work." Ari Aarnihuhta, director, Nina Saarinen, BI manager Customer relations and business intelligence

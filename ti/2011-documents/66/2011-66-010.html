    Crowdsourcing Service Providers: Creative Services

    Although we specialize in crowdsourcing consumer insight at clickadvisor, the biggest growth in new crowdsourcing service providers (CSPs) is currently for creative services.

    I’m helping put together a directory over at http://crowdsourcingexamples.pbworks.com, but here’s an evolving thematic list of creative services that can be crowdsourced online.

    • Graphic Design
      • www.99designs.com
      • www.crowdspring.com
      • www.geniusrocket.com
    • Video Production
      • www.eyeka.com
      • www.geniusrocket.com
      • www.youtube.com/contests_main
    • Photography
      • www.istockphoto.com
      • www.flickr.com/groups/contest_announcements
    • Advertising Creative
      • www.openad.net
      • www.battleofconcepts.com
      • www.bootb.com
      • www.zooppa.com
      • www.votigo.com
    • T-shirt Design
      • www.threadless.com
      • www.spreadshirt.com
    • Fashion Design
      • www.catwalkgenius.com
    • Names
      • www.namethis.com
      • www.namingforce.com
    • Cover Design
      • www.coversourcing.co.uk
    • Copy Editing
      • www.goosegrade.com

      Elastic Lab is another crowd-sourced advertising creative / video production resource. We have a nationwide network of pre-screened filmmakers — who are typically also influential social media participants — that we hire to contribute video footage and to help market the resulting videos. Crowd-sourcing lets us incorporate multiple perspectives, locations, and on-screen subjects at a lower cost than a traditional production company, while letting us create hundreds of videos at a time. (Out of respect for the creatives in our network, we pay fairly for all commissioned footage, even if we don’t use it; this is unlike many other crowd-sourcing companies who run winner-takes-all contests.)

Kirk Boydston's Publications

  • Towards High-Quality Data Extraction via Crowdsourcing

    • AT&T
    • October 2010
    Authors: Kirk Boydston, Donghui Feng, Sveva Besana, Gwen Christian

    Crowdsourcing has recently been attracting increasing attention as a promising means of collecting labels for academic research and industry applications. In this paper, we present our methodology on how to tackle labeling problems using Mechanical Turk (MTurk), including task formulation, HIT (Human Intelligence Task) design and selection, parameter calibration, large-scale data submission, and post-data analysis to extract high-quality data. We also present our findings in the experiments on two MTurk tasks.

  • Have2eat: a Restaurant Finder with Review Summarization for Mobile Phones

    • AT&T
    • 2010
    Authors: Kirk Boydston, Sveva Besana, Giuseppe Di Fabbrizio, Narendra Gupta

    (Contributor)

    Have2eat is a popular mobile application available for iPhone and Android-based devices that helps users to ﬁnd and assess nearby restaurants. It lists restaurants located around the device and provides a quick highlight about the opinions expressed by online reviewers. Have2eat summarizes textual reviews by extracting relevant sentences and by automatically generating detailed ratings about speciﬁc aspects of the restaurant. A compact one-screen digest allows users to quickly access the information they need, expand to full review pages, and report their experience online by entering ratings and comments.

GEO-PICTURES

GEO-PICTURES is ground-breaking space technology. In emergency situations striking humanity, environment and civil public, it is of paramount importance to quickly and accurately communicate field assessments showing what has happened, where, when it happens. This is called situational awareness.

Based on identified gaps in emergency and disaster management, GEO-PICTURES offers the ultimate solution for rapid access to full quality visual and sensor information anywhere anytime by optimizing solutions for satellite communications,  satellite navigation and satellite earth observation.

The project also offers novel solutions for crowdsourcing, allowing the public to share geo-references images quickly after disasters, and the use of unmanned aerial vehicles (UAV).

GEO-PICTURES is the only EU approved project of its kind, and sustainably targets the following fundamental global challenges:

  • Near real-time geo-referenced pictures, video and sensor assessments
  • Rapid mapping via integration with latest satellite Earth Observation images
  • Immediate distribution of images, maps and decisions worldwide

FAQ

What types of business data can CrowdFlower verify?

CrowdFlower's standard Business Listing Verification offering enables clients to verify any or all of the following data elements: company name, address, phone, and URL. Many clients find our solution particularly useful for the acquisition and verification of business URLs. Limited custom configurations are available for additional data fields or elements.

What types of applications are CrowdFlower's Business Listing Verification service best for?

CrowdFlower's Business Listing Verification service is best suited for clients with large sets of standard, verifiable business data for which accuracy and timely updates are critical. Examples of such data include company URL, name, address, and phone number.

I just purchased a list from a qualified data provider. Why do I need to have that data cleaned and updated?

Traditional data collection techniques invariably deliver data with significant inaccuracies. The data further decays the moment it is deployed, as businesses move and close.

How is CrowdFlower's service different from that of business process outsourcers?

We apply proprietary technology and deep expertise to the specific process of listing verification. As well, unlike most outsourcers, CrowdFlower does not employ task workers on a full-time basis. Instead, we maintain quality scores on hundreds of thousands of workers, which improves our ability to enhance data accuracy.

Does CrowdFlower guarantee accuracy rates?

In most circumstances, after viewing a sample of the listings, CrowdFlower can guarantee 90% accuracy of the output data and will usually exceed that. Historically, we have seen results in the range of 95%+ with adequate input data.

How does CrowdFlower achieve higher accuracy and better timeliness of data?

Statistically backed, quality-controlled crowdsourcing technology produces a scalable and reliable workforce that can dispatch a diverse array of tasks and provide near-real time results from workers in the cloud. Each business record is independently checked by multiple crowd workers. Known accurate and inaccurate records are seeded into the workflow to assess judgment quality and worker effectiveness. Ineffective workers are filtered out and workflow is optimized for maximum data accuracy.

How do I get started? What is involved in setup?

Engagements begin with an assessment of client data. If a representative sample data is available, CrowdFlower will require one week or less to evaluate and understand the client data, at which point CrowdFlower will be able to make a quote.

Solutions — Why CrowdFlower?

Ease of Use
CrowdFlower handles all elements of the work from start to finish.

Accuracy
CrowdFlower screens workers continuously by seeding the data with business listings that CrowdFlower already knows are incorrect. Workers who fail to fix these incorrect listings are removed from the pool, and their previous data is discarded.

Using this CrowdFilter™ technology, CrowdFlower can improve the accuracy of a client's data to more than 95% for URLs, addresses, phone numbers, and contact names.

Volume, Scale, and Speed
The CrowdFlower solution can handle up to 1M units of verified listings every month — eclipsing any other labor option.

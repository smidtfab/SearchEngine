Amazon Mechanical Turk

Can I "soft-block" people on Amazon Mechanical Turk?

Is there a way to prevent specific people from completing my HITs without using Amazon's built-in block function? My understanding is that the block function is more or less the "nuclear option" for spammers and other outright subversive workers; I'm trying to find a gentler option for workers who are trying, but just aren't very good at the task. I'd hoped to put all the workers I wanted to block on a qualification task, but there doesn't seem to be a way to say "only allow users without this qualification".

 
 

2 Answers

  
Natala Menezes, product gal for mTurk
1 vote by Jeff McNeill
Unfortunately we don't have this type of feature today. The best workaround is to have a qualification test (not just a qualification) and to start adding good workers (those who are good at your task) to the qualification or to leverage the block function. I know it's not ideal and I'll definitely add your interest on this feature to our product backlog.

The best approach is to use an external HIT approach and check the workerID of the worker. If you do not want the worker to participate in the HIT, then just let the worker know and do not let the worker submit the HIT.

Unfortunately, this approach requires the use of the API, and does not work with the web interface currently available to upload HITs.

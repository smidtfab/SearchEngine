Who coined the term crowdsourcing?

 
 

1 Answer

  
Sam Gopal
4 votes by Marc Bodnick, Panos Ipeirotis, Karim Lakhani and Jake Nickell
Jeff Howe in a 2006 Wired article (http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cro...).  Jeff is a journalist and contributing editor at Wired magazine.  He maintains a current blog on Crowdsourcing (http://www.crowdsourcing.com/) and has also published a book with the same name (http://bit.ly/4Av9iL).

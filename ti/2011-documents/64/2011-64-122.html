FREE MONEY ADVICE N. 60 : How to make money online with Amazon Mechanical Turk

wannamakemoney's site:
www.freemakemoneyadvice.com

http://www.freemakemoneyadvice.com

Amazon Mechanical Turk is an interesting way to make money online : it is a system affiliated with Amazon.com that has been online since 2005.

The Original Mechanical Turk was an artificial artificial intelligence, an Ottoman automaton chess player that beat both Benjamin Franklin and Napoleon Bonaparte in games. Of course, it was operated by an hidden human.

Amazon MTurk ( see Amazon as Affiliate Program related article in archieve ) offers thousands of little jobs that can make you some pennies or few dollars or even Amazon gift certificates ( most tasks take less than a minute to complete).

You can sign in here for free if you want to start earning some extra cash.

You will not probably make more than some dollars an hour, but it is really simple to complete many of the tasks.

Nowaday the level of technology is very high, but there are still some jobs that can be done by humans only.

For istance, computers can not write reviews, take surveys or tell if a photo is pornographic : here MTurk comes in.

Requesters insert jobs called “HITs” ( Human Intelligence Tasks) : you, as worker, can search around these tasks the ones you like or you will be able to accomplish and at the end of each one the requesters will pay you if you complete these HITs.

As every business, you have to be patient at beginning : for some HITs you will need to take a qualifications test to even be allowed to accept the job.

The good news is for each type of task you only have to do this once.

You can then pick your first job and browsing between the price rewards : you may start with a $.02 or less task.

For instance, the job may be look in an image if there is pornographic content and you simply have to click “yes” or “no” , then you will earn the amount promised.

When you find some task that you like, you can keep doing the same job for higher amount of money working for a continued time.

You will discover after some completed tasks, your hourly rate. Simply do the same job for 10 minutes and multiply six times to see your total per hour.

Here below you can find for istance some of the jobs that can be found on Amazon Mechanical Turk :

write articles;
tag images;
transcribe audio or text;
post comments to a blog;
write reviews of products or services;
research details on various topics;
answer few questions;
rate some sites;
check search results;
become a fan of someone facebook page;
tweet a message;
participate in a forum;
bookmark sites;
watch videos.

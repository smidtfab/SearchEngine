Crowdsourcing : Most Dangerous Intersections

Jul 29, 2010 by Henrries Dan

The Fort Green online edition of The NY Times’ The Local has taken an interesting approach to identifying dangerous intersections:  Crowdsourcing.  With the tragic death of Aileen McKay-Dalton, came a barrage of comments pointing to the dangerous nature of that particular Fort Green intersection.

These comments prompted The Local to tap into this knowledge of the community & identify the most dangerous intersections in their city.  Using input from the citizens and Google Maps, several dangerous intersections are being identified.  If you have information on a particularly dangerous intersection, you’re invited to fill out a simple online form.  Identify, based on your own experience, why you feel the intersection is dangerous & offer solutions for making it safer.

Knowledge & statistics of identified intersections will be researched & revealed.  What makes this crowdsourcing exercise valuable is its intention to create solutions to a deadly problem.
